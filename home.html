Step by Step

Follow the steps below to update your email signature in Outlook:

  1. Copy the email signature code from our website or email.
  2. Open Outlook and go to File > Options.
  3. In the Outlook Options window, select Mail from the left sidebar.
  4. Click on the Signatures... button.
  5. In the Email Signature tab, click on New to create a new signature or select an existing signature to update.
  6. In the Edit Signature section, paste the copied email signature code.
  7. Click OK to save the changes.
  8. Compose a new email or reply to an existing email to see your updated email signature.
