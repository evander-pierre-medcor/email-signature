Step by Step

Follow the steps below to update your email signature in Outlook:

  1. Copy the email signature code from our website or email.
  2. Open Outlook and go to File > Options.
  3. In the Outlook Options window, select Mail from the left sidebar.
  4. Click on the Signatures... button.
  5. In the Email Signature tab, click on New to create a new signature.
  6. Enter a name for the signature and click OK.
  7. In the Edit signature section, paste the copied signature code.
  8. Click OK to save your new email signature.
  9. Close the Outlook Options window.

Your email signature is now updated in Outlook! When composing a new email, select the updated signature from the Signature dropdown menu.
